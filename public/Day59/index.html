🔗 Word Chain Challenge

📜 Rules of the Game

  • Player 1 starts by entering any valid word.
  • Next player must enter a word starting with the last letter of the previous word.
  • No word can be repeated.
  • You have 10 seconds to enter a word each turn.
  • If you run out of time or enter an invalid word, you lose!
