Himachal Pradesh Adventures

Thrills in the Land of the Himalayas

Trekking, Snow, and Solitude

Himachal Pradesh is a paradise for trekkers and thrill-seekers alike. From high-altitude snow trails to hidden hamlets and fast rivers, it offers year-round adventure in the lap of the Himalayas.

Adventure Highlights

Triund Trek

A short but scenic trek near McLeod Ganj with stunning Dhauladhar views.

×

Triund Trek – 2 Days

Description: A short yet rewarding trek through rhododendron forests leading to a panoramic ridge with breathtaking views of the Kangra valley and Dhauladhar mountains.

Itinerary:

  • Day 1: Trek from McLeod Ganj to Triund (9 km), passing through oak and rhododendron forests.
  • Evening: Enjoy sunset views, bonfire, and overnight camping under the stars.
  • Day 2: Sunrise hike to explore nearby viewpoints, breakfast at the campsite, and descent back to McLeod Ganj.

Inclusions:

  • Experienced guide for navigation and safety.
  • Camping gear (tents, sleeping bags, mats).
  • Meals (dinner on Day 1, breakfast on Day 2).
  • Entry permits for the trek.
  • Bonfire experience (subject to weather conditions).
  • First aid kit and emergency support.

Spiti Valley Expedition

Explore high-altitude villages, monasteries, and moon-like landscapes.

×

Spiti Valley – 7 Days

Description: Journey to remote Spiti for Buddhist culture, rugged beauty, and ancient gompas.

Itinerary:

  • Day 1: Drive from Shimla to Kalpa, explore Kinnaur Valley.
  • Day 2: Visit Nako Lake, Gue Mummy, and reach Tabo.
  • Day 3: Explore Tabo Monastery, Dhankar Monastery, and Pin Valley.
  • Day 4: Drive to Kaza, visit Key Monastery, Kibber, and Chicham Bridge.
  • Day 5: Trek to Langza, Hikkim (highest post office), and Komic village.
  • Day 6: Drive to Chandratal Lake via Kunzum Pass, camp overnight.
  • Day 7: Return to Manali, concluding the journey.

Inclusions:

  • Transport & permits for seamless travel.
  • Accommodation in hotels/camps.
  • Meals (breakfast & dinner).
  • Experienced guide for cultural insights.
  • Entry fees for monasteries and attractions.
  • Camping gear for Chandratal Lake stay.
  • Safety equipment (first aid kit, oxygen cylinder).

Paragliding in Bir

Fly high above the Kangra valley in India’s paragliding capital.

×

Paragliding at Bir – 4 Days

Description: Experience the thrill of tandem paragliding from Billing (2400m), soaring over the breathtaking Dhauladhar ranges.

Itinerary:

  • Day 1: Arrival at Bir, explore local monasteries and cafes.
  • Day 2: Trek to Billing, enjoy sunset views, and camp overnight.
  • Day 3: Paragliding experience from Billing, landing at Bir.
  • Day 4: Relax, explore Bir market, and depart.

Inclusions:

  • Certified pilot & safety gear for a secure flight.
  • Pickup & drop from Bir to Billing.
  • Flight video to capture your adventure.
  • Accommodation in camps/hotels.
  • Meals (breakfast & dinner).
  • Guided trek to Billing.

Hampta Pass Trek

Cross from lush Kullu valley to arid Lahaul over a stunning high pass.

×

Hampta Pass Trek – 5 Days

Description: A breathtaking crossover trek that connects the lush Kullu Valley to the stark landscapes of Lahaul, featuring glacier streams, alpine meadows, and dramatic mountain passes.

Itinerary:

  • Day 1: Drive from Manali to Jobra, trek to Chika campsite.
  • Day 2: Trek from Chika to Balu Ka Ghera, surrounded by stunning landscapes.
  • Day 3: Trek to Hampta Pass (4,310m), cross over to Shea Goru campsite.
  • Day 4: Trek from Shea Goru to Chhatru, drive to Chandratal Lake (optional).
  • Day 5: Drive back to Manali, concluding the trek.

Inclusions:

  • All trekking permits & environmental fees.
  • Experienced guide & support crew.
  • Meals (vegetarian) throughout the trek.
  • Trekking gear (tents, sleeping bags, mats).
  • Transportation from Manali to base camp & return.
  • Safety equipment (first aid kit, oxygen cylinder).
