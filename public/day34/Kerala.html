Hiking & Trekking in Kerala

Kerala, nestled in the Western Ghats, offers some of the most breathtaking trekking routes in India. Whether it's walking through the misty hills of Munnar, exploring ancient Edakkal Caves in Wayanad, or venturing into the jungles of Thekkady — every step is an adventure.

Adventure Highlights

Munnar Hiking

Walk through rolling tea gardens and scenic viewpoints in Munnar's cool hills.

×

Munnar Hiking – 6 Days

Description: Explore the serene landscapes, tea plantations, and forest trails of Munnar in this 6-day hiking adventure.

Itinerary:

  • Day 1: Arrival and orientation
  • Day 2: Tea plantation hike
  • Day 3: Meesapulimala trek
  • Day 4: Wildlife sanctuary visit
  • Day 5: Cultural exploration
  • Day 6: Departure

Inclusions:

  • Accommodation
  • All meals
  • Guided treks
  • Transportation

Conclusion:

This trek offers a perfect balance of adventure, nature, and culture in the breathtaking hills of Munnar.

Wayanad Trek

Discover waterfalls, caves, and tropical forests on this lush green trek.

×

Wayanad Trek – 7 Days

Description: Embark on an unforgettable journey through Wayanad's misty hills, lush forests, and historic caves.

Itinerary:

  • Day 1: Arrival in Wayanad & Orientation
  • Day 2: Edakkal Caves and heritage sites
  • Day 3: Soochipara Waterfalls
  • Day 4: Chembra Peak rainforest trek
  • Day 5: Village walk and cuisine tour
  • Day 6: Leisure and local nature tour
  • Day 7: Departure

Inclusions:

  • 6 nights camp/lodging
  • Meals during the trek
  • Certified local guide
  • Transportation & entry fees

Conclusion:

Ideal for nature lovers and adventure seekers, this trek offers an immersive escape in Kerala’s wild beauty.

Periyar Wildlife Adventure

Go bamboo rafting, jungle trekking, and spot wildlife in the Periyar Tiger Reserve.

×

Periyar Wildlife Adventure – 6 Days

Description: Dive deep into Kerala’s famed Periyar Tiger Reserve. Enjoy thrilling jungle treks, bamboo rafting, and immersive wildlife experiences.

Itinerary:

  • Day 1: Arrival and orientation
  • Day 2: Guided jungle walk & night camping
  • Day 3: Bamboo rafting and lake exploration
  • Day 4: Wildlife safari and bird watching
  • Day 5: Cultural tour of tribal villages
  • Day 6: Departure

Inclusions:

  • Accommodation in eco-lodges
  • All meals included
  • Park entry & activity fees
  • Expert forest guide and transport

Conclusion:

This adventure is perfect for those seeking a raw and rich wildlife experience in the heart of Kerala’s jungles.

Agastyarkoodam Peak

Trek to one of the highest peaks in Kerala, sacred and full of rare biodiversity.

×

Agastyarkoodam Peak Trek – 8 Days

Description: Challenge yourself with an epic trek to Agastyarkoodam — a UNESCO Biosphere Reserve known for sacred legends, rare herbs, and untouched forest trails.

Itinerary:

  • Day 1: Arrival and orientation in Trivandrum
  • Day 2: Permit formalities and cultural tour
  • Day 3: Transfer to base camp
  • Day 4: Trek to Athirumala
  • Day 5: Summit Agastyarkoodam and return to base
  • Day 6: Rest and visit nearby tribal areas
  • Day 7: Visit Neyyar Dam and relax
  • Day 8: Departure

Inclusions:

  • Permit handling & entry fees
  • Accommodation and meals
  • Certified local guide and support staff
  • Transport from Trivandrum

Conclusion:

A spiritually enriching and physically rewarding trek, Agastyarkoodam is a must-do for serious trekkers and nature lovers.
