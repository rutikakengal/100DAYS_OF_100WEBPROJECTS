Sikkim Adventures

Trekking through the Eastern Himalayas

Trekking & Exploration in Sikkim

Nestled in the Eastern Himalayas, Sikkim offers surreal landscapes, alpine lakes, sacred monasteries, and exhilarating trekking trails. From Dzongri to Goechala, it's an adventurer's paradise.

Adventure Highlights

Dzongri Trek

Short yet stunning trek through rhododendron forests to majestic mountain views.

×

Dzongri Trek – 5 Days

Description: Ideal for those with limited time, the Dzongri Trek offers Himalayan views without the longer commitment of Goechala.

Itinerary:

  • Day 1: Yuksom to Tshoka
  • Day 2: Tshoka to Dzongri
  • Day 3: Explore Dzongri La
  • Day 4: Return to Tshoka
  • Day 5: Back to Yuksom

Inclusions:

  • Guides and porters
  • Tents/guesthouse stay
  • All meals during trek

Perfect for intermediate trekkers seeking scenic mountain experiences.

Goechala Trek

Epic journey to a high Himalayan pass with views of Kanchenjunga.

×

Goechala Trek – 10 Days

Description: A high-altitude trek offering jaw-dropping views of Mt. Kanchenjunga.

Itinerary Highlights:

  • Yuksom – Tshoka – Dzongri – Thansing – Lamuney – Goechala Viewpoint

Inclusions:

  • Experienced guides & porters
  • Tent accommodation
  • Meals & permits

A true Himalayan adventure for experienced trekkers.

Green Lake Trek

Less-traveled trail to a mystical glacial lake near Mt. Kanchenjunga base.

×

Green Lake Trek – 12 Days

Description: Offbeat trek to the alpine Green Lake, offering solitude and mountain drama.

Inclusions:

  • All logistics and permits
  • Tent camping
  • Meals and experienced guides

Ideal for seasoned trekkers looking for raw Himalayan beauty.

Barsey Rhododendron Trek

Springtime trek through colorful rhododendron forests and alpine views.

×

Barsey Rhododendron Trek – 4 Days

Description: A scenic trek through the Barsey Rhododendron Sanctuary, offering breathtaking views of blooming rhododendrons and the Kangchenjunga range.

Itinerary:

  • Day 1: Drive from Gangtok to Hilley, trek to Barsey, and settle into eco-camp.
  • Day 2: Explore the sanctuary, witness vibrant rhododendron blooms, and enjoy panoramic mountain views.
  • Day 3: Trek to nearby viewpoints, spot rare birds, and visit local villages.
  • Day 4: Return trek to Hilley, drive back to Gangtok, and conclude the journey.

Inclusions:

  • Eco-camp stay for a comfortable experience in nature.
  • Experienced guide for navigation and insights into flora and fauna.
  • Meals featuring local delicacies.
  • Permits & logistics for hassle-free trekking.
  • Entry fees for the sanctuary.
  • Safety equipment (first aid kit, trekking poles).

Perfect short trek during spring with rich flora and fauna.
