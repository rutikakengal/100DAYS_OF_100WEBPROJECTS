Uttarakhand Treks

Explore the Himalayan trails and hidden gems of Devbhumi

Trekking in Uttarakhand

Uttarakhand, nestled in the Indian Himalayas, offers some of the most stunning and spiritually uplifting trekking experiences in the country. From alpine meadows to glacial lakes, it's an adventurer's paradise.

Adventure Highlights

Valley of Flowers

Experience vibrant alpine blooms and Himalayan vistas in this UNESCO heritage site.

×

Valley of Flowers – 6 Days

Description: Trek through UNESCO-listed alpine meadows with stunning flora and panoramic views of snow-capped peaks.

Itinerary:

  • Day 1: Arrival in Govindghat
  • Day 2: Trek to Ghangaria
  • Day 3: Explore Valley of Flowers
  • Day 4: Visit Hemkund Sahib
  • Day 5: Return to Govindghat
  • Day 6: Departure

Inclusions:

  • Accommodation & meals
  • Certified trek leader
  • Permits & transportation

Conclusion:

A perfect mix of nature, tranquility, and altitude for flower enthusiasts and casual trekkers alike.

Kedarkantha Trek

A classic winter trek offering snow-covered trails and breathtaking sunrise views.

×

Kedarkantha Trek – 5 Days

Description: Climb through pine forests and snowy trails to reach a summit with panoramic Himalayan views.

Itinerary:

  • Day 1: Arrival at Sankri
  • Day 2: Trek to Juda Ka Talab
  • Day 3: Base Camp to Kedarkantha Summit
  • Day 4: Return to Sankri
  • Day 5: Departure

Inclusions:

  • Tent accommodation & meals
  • Experienced trek leader
  • Gear and permits

Conclusion:

A great beginner-to-intermediate trek for experiencing snow and summits in a short span.

Roopkund Trek

Adventure into the high-altitude mystery lake surrounded by glaciers and folklore.

×

Roopkund Trek – 8 Days

Description: A legendary trek to the glacial lake with human skeletal remains, through forests and alpine meadows.

Itinerary:

  • Day 1: Arrival at Lohajung
  • Day 2–3: Trek through Didna and Bedni Bugyal
  • Day 4–5: Trek to Bhagwabasa and Roopkund
  • Day 6–7: Return trek
  • Day 8: Departure

Inclusions:

  • Camping gear, permits
  • Meals & guides
  • Transport from Kathgodam

Conclusion:

Ideal for experienced trekkers looking for a challenging yet beautiful Himalayan expedition.

Nanda Devi Base Camp

Witness the majesty of India’s second-highest peak on this challenging journey.

×

Nanda Devi Base Camp – 10 Days

Description: Trek through remote villages and alpine routes to the base of one of the most sacred and awe-inspiring peaks in the Himalayas.

Itinerary:

  • Day 1: Arrive in Munsyari
  • Day 2–4: Trek through Lilam, Bog Udyari, Martoli
  • Day 5–6: Advance to Nanda Devi Base
  • Day 7–9: Return trek
  • Day 10: Departure

Inclusions:

  • Experienced guide & porters
  • All meals and camping equipment
  • Transport to/from base village

Conclusion:

A demanding and rewarding trek for seasoned trekkers with a love for high Himalayas.
