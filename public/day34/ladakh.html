Trekking & Exploration in Ladakh

Ladakh offers an unparalleled adventure through high-altitude deserts, monasteries, frozen rivers, and majestic Himalayan ranges. From the legendary Chadar Trek to the tranquil Nubra Valley, experience nature's raw beauty.

Adventure Highlights

Chadar Frozen River Trek

Walk across a frozen river surrounded by towering cliffs of ice.

×

Chadar Trek – 9 Days

Description: A surreal winter expedition over the frozen Zanskar River in Ladakh, famed for its icy terrain and ancient trails.

Itinerary:

  • Day 1: Arrival in Leh
  • Day 2-3: Acclimatization
  • Day 4-7: Trek across frozen Zanskar
  • Day 8: Return to Leh
  • Day 9: Departure

Inclusions:

  • Permits & guide
  • Winter gear rental
  • Meals & tents

A once-in-a-lifetime icy trek for thrill-seekers and photographers.

Markha Valley Trek

Traverse ancient villages, rivers, and high passes in this scenic trek.

×

Markha Valley Trek – 7 Days

Description: A breathtaking trail through the Hemis National Park and over high-altitude passes.

Itinerary:

  • Day 1: Leh to Chilling
  • Day 2-6: Trek via Markha, Thachungtse, Nimaling
  • Day 7: Return to Leh

Inclusions:

  • All permits & camping gear
  • Support crew & meals

Perfect for trekkers looking for culture, wildlife, and alpine beauty.

Nubra Valley Exploration

Experience dunes, monasteries, and camel rides in a cold desert.

×

Nubra Valley Exploration – 5 Days

Description: A road trip to the northernmost valley of India, famous for double-humped camels, Diskit Monastery, and Pangong Lake add-ons.

Itinerary:

  • Day 1: Leh to Diskit
  • Day 2: Hunder & camel ride
  • Day 3: Visit monasteries
  • Day 4: Optional Pangong Lake
  • Day 5: Return to Leh

Inclusions:

  • Transport & accommodation
  • Meals and local guide

A gentle yet magical journey through Ladakh’s cultural side.
