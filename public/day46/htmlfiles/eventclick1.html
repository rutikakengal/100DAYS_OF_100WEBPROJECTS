A Two-Day Workshop on IoT & Its Applications

Organized by St. Peter's Engineering College

Date: September 1-2, 2023

Venue: F-44

Topics and Discussion

  • Introduction to IoT
  • Raspberry Pi

About the Workshop:

This workshop will cover the basics of IoT and its applications. Participants will learn about the Internet of Things, its components, and how to use the Raspberry Pi for IoT projects.
